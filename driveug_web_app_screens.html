Verification centre
Owner verification
All listings reviewed before going live. Usually within 24 hours.
  • National ID uploaded and matched
  • Vehicle logbook verified
  • Insurance certificate valid
  • Phone number confirmed
  • Car photos reviewed — pending
Renter requirements
Renters provide this before their first booking is confirmed.
  • National ID or passport
  • Valid driving permit
  • Security deposit held (released after return)
  • Optional damage protection cover
All payments are held securely by DriveUG and only released to the owner after the car is returned safely. Disputes are handled by our team.
